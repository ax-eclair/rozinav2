Project name: Collection Red
Sector: Art Direction

A Berlin-based collaborative photoshoot brought together creative female talents to highlight key pieces from my past collections. Featuring a striking red knitted dress and an upcycled denim jacket, the shoot explores the interplay between Berliner vibes and sustainable fashion. Captured through the lens of Franciska Legát, styled with garments from W1P Studios by Weronika on Lara Bartos, the project blends character and conscious design in a realistic-Berlin setting.

Photographer: Franciska Legat
Styling: Nika Gniewowska
Model: Lara Bartos
Knitted Red Dress, Denim vest by: Rozina Pusztai
