ROZINA PUSZTAI

Combining a foundation in textile design and art & design management, I bring a multifaceted approach as an art director and stylist specializing in e-commerce, film, advertising, and social media campaigns. With a keen visual sensibility and strong attention to detail, I blend conceptual clarity with a hands-on, material-driven process. For over three years, and counting, I’ve maintained a trusted freelance relationship with a leading global publishing firm, even while studying and working abroad. This ongoing collaboration reflects the long-term commitment, consistency, and client-focused mindset I bring to every project.

INTERNATIONAL PERSPECTIVE

Born in Budapest, currently based in Berlin, and having lived in Denmark, Australia, Germany, the United States, and Italy — experiences that continue to enrich my visual language and shape my working style.

CREATIVE SKILLS & SIDE PROJECTS

Social media management, copywriting, costume design, art direction, fashion sketching, oil painting, and illustration.
